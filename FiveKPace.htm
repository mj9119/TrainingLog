Calculate 5K pace per mile. Or determine whether you speeded up or slowed down during your 5K race.

  • To calculate overall 5k pace per mile: Enter a number for Minutes and for Seconds where the screen reads in red: "Finishing time is mandatory". Then click the "Calculate average pace per mile" button.

  • To determine whether you speeded up or slowed down in your 5k, based upon known clock times: Enter your finishing time in Minutes and Seconds as described in the first bullet point. Then, enter clock times in Minutes & Seconds for known mile splits. For example, if you ran 24:30 for 5k, enter a finishing time of 24 and 30 as described in the first sentence of the first bullet point (above). Then, let's say you remember only that your time at the two mile mark was 16:10. In that case, on the line where the screen reads: "Enter Mile 2 clock time if known", enter 16 in the Minutes column and 10 in the Seconds column. Then, click the "Calculate average pace per mile" button to see that you speeded up to 7:31 pace on the last portion of the 3.1 mile race.
